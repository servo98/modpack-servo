# Sistema de Jewelry

> Fuente: GDD v2, seccion 3.13

> Relacionado: [RPG Classes](rpg-classes.md), [Accessories](accessories.md), [Dungeons](dungeons.md)
> Datos: docs/mod-data/rpg-series-content.md

## Overview

85 items: gem rings, gem necklaces, unique loot-only jewelry. 6 gemas custom con ores underground.

## Gemas

6 tipos de gema con ores que spawnan underground (no biome-locked):
- Citrine, Jade, Ruby, Sapphire, Tanzanite, Topaz

## Desbloqueo por capitulo

| Cap | Disponible | Gemas |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Copper Ring, Iron Ring | Ninguna |
| 2 | Gold Ring + Citrine/Jade Ring y Necklace | Citrine, Jade |
| 3 | + Ruby/Sapphire Ring y Necklace, Jeweler's Kit | Ruby, Sapphire |
| 4 | + Tanzanite/Topaz, Diamond, Emerald | Tanzanite, Topaz |
| 5 | + Netherite Gem jewelry (todas variantes) | Netherite + cualquier gema |
| 6+ | Todo crafteable. Unique solo de dungeon | - |
