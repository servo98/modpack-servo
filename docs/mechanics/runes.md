# Sistema de Runas

> Fuente: GDD v2, seccion 3.13, 4.2
> Mod: Runes (RPG Series by Daedelus)
> Relacionado: [RPG Classes](rpg-classes.md), [Dungeons](dungeons.md)

## Overview

Runas consumibles que potencian spells. 6 tipos basicos + 2 avanzados. Se llevan en Rune Pouches.

## Tipos de runa

| Runa | Tipo | Disponible |
|------|------|------------|
| Arcane | Basica | Ch1 |
| Frost | Basica | Ch1 |
| Fire | Basica | Ch2 |
| Healing | Basica | Ch2 |
| Soul | Avanzada | Ch5 |
| Lightning | Avanzada | Ch5 |

## Rune Pouches

| Pouch | Capacidad | Disponible | Material especial |
|-------|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| Small Rune Pouch | Basica | Ch1 | - |
| Medium Rune Pouch | Media | Ch3 | - |
| Large Rune Pouch | Grande | Ch5 | Nether Star (Wither) |

## Crafteo

| Metodo | Cantidad | Disponible |
|--------|----------|------------|
| A mano | x2 o x4 | Ch1+ |
| Rune Crafting Altar | x8 o x16 | Ch3+ |
